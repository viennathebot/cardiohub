## How Trials Move to Landmark Status

### Criteria for Landmark
1. **Practice-changing** — altered guidelines or standard of care
2. **Methodologically rigorous** — RCT, adequate power, appropriate endpoints
3. **Replicated or confirmed** — or foundational if novel
4. **High citation impact** — recognized by field
5. **Teaching value** — illustrates important principle

### Graduation Process
1. Appears in Recent Trials
2. Monitored for 6-12 months
3. If meets criteria → moves to main Landmark database
4. Full write-up created with all sections
