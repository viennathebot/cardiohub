## Friday Research: Best Cardiology Education Platforms 2026

### Top Platforms Identified

#### 1. ESC Online Learning (escardio.org)
- Interactive web-based courses, webinars, congress presentations
- Subspecialty training: core → specialty → expert levels
- CME credits included
- **Emulate:** Tiered learning paths, case-integrated webinars

#### 2. SCAI (scai.org)
- U.S. society for interventional cardiology
- Clinical guidelines, education, career-stage resources
- **Emulate:** Career-stage progression system

#### 3. ACC Education (acc.org)
- ACC Anywhere video library
- ECG drill/practice tools
- Auscultation cases
- **Emulate:** ECG practice interface, audio-based learning

#### 4. BoardVitals Cardiology CME
- 1400+ case-style questions with rationales
- 65 AMA PRA Category 1 Credits
- ABIM MOC points
- **Emulate:** Question bank format, detailed rationales
